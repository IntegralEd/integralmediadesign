Selected Case Studies

Real projects that demonstrate how we help mission-driven organizations communicate more effectively

Statewide Education Conference Keynote

Challenge

A statewide nonprofit needed to redesign their annual conference keynote presentation. The existing deck contained dense policy research that was difficult for attendees to follow and remember.

Approach

We partnered with their leadership team to translate the policy research into a 60-slide narrative anchored by visual metaphors and data storytelling. Our approach prioritized clarity, pacing, and audience engagement.

Outcomes

  • Increased post-session engagement and follow-up requests
  • Reusable slide template adopted across departments
  • Clearer funder-facing communications

Public Awareness PSA Campaign

Challenge

A youth-serving organization needed to raise public awareness about their initiative through video media. They required a flagship narrative piece plus social media adaptations for partners to distribute.

Approach

We produced a short-form video series that included a flagship 3-minute narrative film and six 30-second social adaptations. Our team handled scripting, filming, editing, motion graphics, and accessible captioning.

Outcomes

  • Increased social engagement across partner networks
  • Expanded partner distribution beyond initial targets
  • Accessible captioned versions used by school districts

Integrated Media Toolkit for Program Launch

Challenge

A multiregional nonprofit was launching a new initiative and needed a cohesive set of media assets to support rollout across regions. They required materials that could be adapted locally while maintaining a consistent brand narrative.

Approach

We designed an integrated launch toolkit including presentation decks, one-pagers, landing page visuals, and digital campaign assets. The system was designed for consistency at scale while allowing regional customization.

Outcomes

  • Consistent brand narrative maintained across regions
  • Faster regional rollout timelines
  • Improved stakeholder clarity and alignment
